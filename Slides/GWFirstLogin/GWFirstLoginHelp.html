Login Help

  • To Login to G-Wizard software, you'll need an email that has already been registered either for a free trial or was the email you gave when you purchased the software.

 

  • Just enter that email into the prompt on the popup and press the "Login" button.

 

  • If G-Wizard won't recognize your email, press the "Register" button on the popup to register for a trial or to purchase the software.

    If you're sure you're already registered, make sure you haven't mispelled the email. For more help, just press the "Help" button up above.

 

  • Use the chat thingey to the lower right if all else fails. We want to help!

 

What are those Google and Social Login Thingies?

Social Login can save you time. Assuming you used the same email to register your G-Wizard software that you use for the Social Network (like Google, Facebook, or LinkedIn), just click the button to login.

Note: If you ever need to change your login email, for example so it matches your Social Login email, just drop us a note and we'll help you get it changed.
