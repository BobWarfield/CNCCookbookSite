What's A Beta Test?

Beta Tests are opportunities for Software Developers and Users to collaborate. We make the software available for free and call it a "Beta Test." You get something cool for no cost, but in exchange we want your feedback and your patience. There will be bugs in Beta Software, that's par for the course. In fact, since the code is being used for the first time, there's probably more bugs than at any other time in a product's history.

Beta Testing is not for everyone, but if you're patient and willing to provide feedback, it can offer you a lot of value.
