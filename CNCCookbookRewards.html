Welcome to the CNCCookbook Rewards Program!

 

Do you know what makes me so excited about CNC?

I just love the productivity.  I’d been a manual machinist for years before taking up CNC.  I’d heard all the stuff about how it was only good if you wanted to make 10 of some part and not for one offs.  But here’s what I found:

CNC is to manual machining as word processing is to a manual typewriter.

I love a sweet Monarch 10EE as much as the next guy. But CNC is just fabulously more productive, especially if you don't have the years of experience that make a good manual machinist the consumate craftsman that he is. For most of us, the end result from CNC comes out looking much nicer, and with much less effort.  I was so happy I’d found it, almost from the very first part I made on my CNC.

Since that first part, I’ve worked hard to help others realize the benefits of CNC.  The motto of my CNCCookbook business is, “Be a Better CNC’er!”  That’s exactly what I want to do—help as many people as possible, at all skill levels, to be better CNC’ers.

For some folks, CNC has turned into a valuable business.  For others, it’s a fun hobby.  Still others have made it an artform, and their craftsmanship and creativity are amazing!

That’s okay!  What’s important is that each offer be around products that are special and that the deal we bring you is also very special.  Because of that, we’re going to call this the “CNCCookbook Rewards Program.”  It’s sort of like Groupon and similar programs, but it’s aimed at CNC’ers.
