Optimize Speeds and Feeds Using G-Wizard's Cut Optimizer

It takes a lot of homework to do great CNC work. Take the task of optimizing feeds and speeds before you can start making chips. There's a world of performance available beyond the basic SFM and IPT calculations all machinists know by heart. I found myself wasting way too much time calculating and preparing when I wanted to be making chips, and I kept coming across articles and information that showed me how to do it better with still more homework. So, I created G-Wizard, a full-featured Machinist's calculator that does a lot of the homework for you:

  

Full Featured Scientific Calculator:

  • Fractions
  • Trig functions
  • Unit conversions plus angles in degrees, radians, or degrees/minutes/seconds.

 

 

 
 

Feeds and Speeds for all common turning, milling, and drilling operations:

  • Compensation for chip thinning, lead angle, depth of cut, and ballnosed cutters.
  • Deep hole drilling recommendations including support for parabolic drills.
  • Reamer hole size recommendations.
  • Conventional vs Climb milling recommendations.
  • Micro-cutter Feed and Speed compensation for cutters less than 1/16" in diameter.
  • Compensation for cutting without coolant.
  • Integrated with the Tool Crib and Machine Profiles. Customize for your tools and machine HP, spindle rpm, and feedrates.
  • Integrated with G-Wizard G-Code Editor. Calculate feeds and speeds and directly output g-codes.

 
 

Innovative Cut Optimizer:

  • Optimize your depth and width of cut
  • Analyzes Tool Deflection
  • Powerful iterative solver
  • Figure best stepover or level depth when pocketing and profiling
  • Settings for roughing and finishing
  • Minimizing deflection increases accuracy and improves surface finish

 

 

 
 

Geometry Calculators:

  • Right and Oblique Triangles
  • Bolt circles
  • Dovetails
  • Tapers
  • Chamfers
  • Chords
  • True Position
  • Even a Turner's Cube Calculator!

 
 

Thread Reference:

  • Dimensions Shown on Drawings
  • Unified, ISO Metric, and NPT Pipe Threads
  • Internal and External Threads
  • Tapping Drill Sizes with % of Thread
  • Both Cutting and Form Taps

 

 
 

Quick Reference Charts and Tables:

  • Drill Index
  • Socket Head and Flat Head Cap Screws
  • Weights, Volumes, and Costs based on Material and Shape
  • Standard Fabrication Sizes (e.g. I-Beam in W8x28)
  • G and M-Code Reference for CNC'ers
  • Hardness Conversion Charts
  • Rigidity Calculator to Compare Rigidity of Different Tooling

 
 

Setup Customizes G-Wizard for Your Machines and Tooling:

  • Create Machine Profiles
  • Imperial and Metric Units Throughout G-Wizard
  • Create custom Tool Cribs for machines, tool changers, tool carts, and other tooling inventory.
