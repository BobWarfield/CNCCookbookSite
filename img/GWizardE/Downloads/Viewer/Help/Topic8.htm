Sectioning

The part can be sectioned to see invisible elements.  CutViewer supports two modes of sectioning, "Parallel to Axis" and 'Through 2 points". 

«Parallel to Axis" generates a sectioned part slicing it with a plane parallel to the Axis.  Press the "Parallel to Axis" button and an outline of the top edges of the stock will appears.  Place the mouse cursor on one of the lines and drag it to the location where you would like to see the section cut. When you release the mouse an outline of the section will appear below the line. You may move the section line again with the mouse. Repeat this for other edges if necessary.  When finally satisfied, press the right mouse button to create the section.

"Through 2 points section"- allows you to cut away a portion of the part through any vertical plane defined by two points.  This option is available only on the Top and ISO views.  Move the cursor to the first position and press the left mouse button down.  While holding the button pull a "rubber band line" to the desired position.  Release the mouse button and correct the position of the points as necessary.  When you are satisfied with the position of the section line, move the cursor to the side of part you want to remove and press the right mouse button. 

To restore the sectioned part press the "Unsectioning" button or choose the menu item 'Section', 'Unsectioning'.
